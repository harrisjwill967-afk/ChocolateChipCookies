Chocolate Chip Cookie recipe

These classic chocolate chip cookies are soft, chewy, and easy to make at home.

This recipe bakes 24 cookies and takes less than 30 minutes
Ingredients Flour Sugar Eggs Salt Water Vanilla Baking Soda Chocolate Chip
Amount 3 cups 1 cup white
1 cup brown		 2 1/2 Tsp. 2 Tsp. 2 Tsp. 1 Tsp. 2 cups

Step by Step baking process

  1. Preheat the oven to 350℉.
  2. Beat the butter and sugars then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  3. Mix the baking soda with hot water and add it to the mixture.
  4. Whisk in the Flour and then add the chocolate chips.
  5. Shape the cookie dough into balls and place them onto a baking sheet.
  6. Place the baking sheet into the oven and bake for 10 minutes at 350℉.
